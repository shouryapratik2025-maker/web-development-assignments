Chocolate Brownies

Fudgy, rich and gooey chocolate brownies with a perfectly crispy top

⏱ Prep: 15 min 🍳 Bake: 25 min ⭐ Difficulty: Medium 👤 Serves: 9 pieces

🛒 Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup (115g) butter
  • 1 cup (200g) sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup (40g) cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup (65g) all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips (optional)

📋 Step-by-Step Instructions

1

Preheat your oven to 175°C (350°F). Grease a square 8x8 inch baking pan with butter or line it with parchment paper.

2

Melt the butter in a saucepan over low heat or in a microwave. Let it cool for a few minutes before using.

3

In a large bowl, mix the melted butter and sugar together until well combined. Add the eggs and vanilla extract, and beat everything together.

4

Sift in the cocoa powder, flour, salt, and baking powder. Fold gently with a spatula until just combined. Do not overmix.

5

If using chocolate chips, fold them into the batter now. Pour the batter into the prepared baking pan and spread it evenly.

6

Bake in the preheated oven for 20 to 25 minutes. Check with a toothpick — it should come out with a few moist crumbs (not wet batter).

7

Let the brownies cool completely in the pan before cutting them into squares. Serve with vanilla ice cream or enjoy on their own!
