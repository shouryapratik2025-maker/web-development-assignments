Fluffy Pancakes

Golden, soft and fluffy pancakes — the perfect breakfast treat

⏱ Prep: 5 min 🍳 Cook: 10 min ⭐ Difficulty: Easy 👤 Serves: 4

🛒 Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup buttermilk (or milk)
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • Maple syrup for serving

📋 Step-by-Step Instructions

1

In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Whisk them together so there are no lumps.

2

In a separate small bowl, whisk together the buttermilk, egg, and melted butter until well mixed.

3

Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir gently. Do not over-mix — a few lumps in the batter are fine and actually help make the pancakes fluffy.

4

Heat a non-stick pan or griddle over medium heat. Add a small amount of butter to grease the surface.

5

Pour about 1/4 cup of batter onto the pan for each pancake. Cook until bubbles form on the surface (about 2 minutes), then flip and cook for 1 more minute until golden brown.

6

Serve the pancakes warm with maple syrup, fresh fruits, or any toppings you like. Enjoy your breakfast!
