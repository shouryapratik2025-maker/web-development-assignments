Chicken Biryani

Aromatic spiced rice layered with tender, marinated chicken — a true classic

⏱ Prep: 20 min 🍳 Cook: 40 min ⭐ Difficulty: Medium 👤 Serves: 4

🛒 Ingredients

  • 500g chicken (cut into pieces)
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 2 large onions (sliced)
  • 1 cup yoghurt
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 2 tablespoons biryani masala
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chilli powder
  • 4 tablespoons oil or ghee
  • Fresh mint and coriander
  • Salt to taste
  • 4 cups water

📋 Step-by-Step Instructions

1

Wash the basmati rice and soak it in water for 30 minutes. This helps the rice cook evenly and become fluffy.

2

Marinate the chicken with yoghurt, turmeric, red chilli powder, biryani masala, and salt. Mix well and let it sit for at least 20 minutes.

3

Heat oil in a deep pot. Fry the sliced onions until they turn golden brown and crispy. Remove half of them and set aside for garnish.

4

Add the marinated chicken to the pot with the remaining onions. Cook on medium heat for 10 to 12 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the chicken is nearly cooked.

5

Add the chopped tomatoes and cook for another 5 minutes until the oil separates from the masala.

6

Drain the soaked rice and layer it on top of the chicken. Add 4 cups of water, cover the pot tightly, and cook on low heat for 20 minutes until the rice is fully cooked.

7

Garnish with the reserved fried onions, fresh mint, and coriander leaves. Serve hot with raita or salad.
