Creamy Garlic Pasta

A simple and delicious pasta dish made with a buttery garlic cream sauce

⏱ Prep: 5 min 🍳 Cook: 15 min ⭐ Difficulty: Easy 👤 Serves: 2

🛒 Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti or pasta
  • 4 cloves of garlic (minced)
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese
  • Salt to taste
  • Black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley (for garnish)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

📋 Step-by-Step Instructions

1

Boil a large pot of salted water. Add the pasta and cook it according to the packet instructions until it is al dente (firm to bite). Save 1/2 cup of the pasta water before draining.

2

While the pasta is cooking, heat the butter and olive oil together in a large pan on medium heat. Add the minced garlic and fry it for about 1 to 2 minutes. Be careful not to burn the garlic.

3

Pour the heavy cream into the pan with the garlic. Stir well and let it simmer on low heat for 3 to 4 minutes until the sauce thickens slightly.

4

Add the parmesan cheese and stir until it melts into the sauce. Season with salt and black pepper according to your taste.

5

Add the drained pasta to the pan and toss it gently with the sauce. If the sauce is too thick, add a little saved pasta water to loosen it up.

6

Serve immediately in bowls. Garnish with fresh chopped parsley and extra parmesan cheese on top. Enjoy your meal!
