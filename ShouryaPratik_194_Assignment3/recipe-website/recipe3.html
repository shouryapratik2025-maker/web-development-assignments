Greek Salad

Fresh, crunchy and healthy — loaded with veggies, olives and feta cheese

⏱ Prep: 10 min 🍳 Cook: 0 min ⭐ Difficulty: Easy 👤 Serves: 2

🛒 Ingredients

  • 2 large tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 cucumber (chopped)
  • 1 red onion (sliced)
  • 1 green bell pepper (chopped)
  • 100g feta cheese (cubed)
  • 1/2 cup black olives
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

📋 Step-by-Step Instructions

1

Wash all the vegetables thoroughly under clean running water. This is an important hygiene step before you start chopping.

2

Chop the tomatoes, cucumber, and green bell pepper into medium-sized chunks. Slice the red onion into thin rings or half-rings.

3

Place all the chopped vegetables and the black olives into a large salad bowl. Toss them together gently.

4

In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper to make the dressing.

5

Pour the dressing over the salad and toss everything together so all the vegetables are coated evenly.

6

Place the cubed feta cheese on top of the salad. Serve immediately or refrigerate for 15 minutes for a cooler, more flavourful salad.
