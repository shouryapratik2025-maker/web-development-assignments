About This Blog

Who we are and why we write about technology.

Hi, I'm Alex — the author behind TechMuse.

I'm a second-year Computer Science student passionate about making technology understandable for everyone. I started TechMuse as part of my CS106 Web Design course, but it quickly grew into something I genuinely love.

My goal is to cut through the noise and explain complex tech topics in plain, honest language — without buzzwords or hype. Whether you're a developer, a curious beginner, or just someone who uses a smartphone, there's something here for you.

When I'm not writing or coding, you'll find me hiking, playing chess, or obsessively reading about space exploration.
